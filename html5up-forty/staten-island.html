Staten Island

Open Garden Days: Alice's Garden at the H.H. Biddle House at H.H. Biddle House (in Conference House Park), Staten Island This is an Accessible location 7:00 a.m.–7:00 p.m. August 2nd The Tottenville Historical Society invites you to visit Alice’s Garden, an arrangement of rare and unique architectural terra cotta ornaments.

Forest Adventures at Greenbelt Nature Center (in Blood Root Valley), Staten Island This is an Accessible location 10:00 a.m.–11:00 a.m. August 2nd This weekly series is filled with woodland adventures for children aged 3.5 - 5 years.

Arts, Culture & Fun: Lucia Rappa: Au Naturel at Conference House Park Visitor Center (in Conference House Park), Staten Island This is an Accessible location 10:00 a.m.–4:00 p.m. August 2nd Artist Lucia Rappa’s exhibition “Au Naturel” explores the contrasts and similarities between the human form and nature.
