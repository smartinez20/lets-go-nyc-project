About Us

Co-founders Julian Coley and Stephanie Martinez created Let'sGO NYC for the people who never know what to do during their free time. Living in New York City we know how hard it is to find things to do other than go to Manhattan, so we decided to create a website where low cost unknown events can be posted and you can attend.
